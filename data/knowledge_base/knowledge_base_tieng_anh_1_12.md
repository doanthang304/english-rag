**Cấu trúc câu:**

> Tom: I'm fine, thanks. Goodbye!
> Ann: Bye, Tom!

| do homework | /duː ˈhoʊmwɜːrk/ | làm bài tập |
| have dinner | /hæv ˈdɪnər/ | ăn tối |
| go to bed | /ɡoʊ tə bed/ | đi ngủ |

**Ngữ pháp: Simple Present (Thì hiện tại đơn)**
- Dùng để diễn tả thói quen, hoạt động hàng ngày
- Công thức: S + V(s/es) + O
- I wake up at 6 o'clock every morning. → Tôi thức dậy lúc 6 giờ mỗi sáng.
- She goes to school at 7 a.m. → Cô ấy đi học lúc 7 giờ sáng.
- He brushes his teeth after meals. → Anh ấy đánh răng sau bữa ăn.

**Quy tắc thêm -s/-es:**
- Phần lớn: + s (work → works)
- Tận cùng -o, -s, -x, -z, -ch, -sh: + es (go → goes, watch → watches)
- Tận cùng phụ âm + y: y → ies (study → studies)

### Lớp 5 – Unit 3: Comparatives & Superlatives (So sánh)

**So sánh hơn (Comparative):**
| Tính từ ngắn | Tính từ dài | Bất quy tắc |
|-------------|------------|------------|
| adj + -er + than | more + adj + than | good → better |
| tall → taller than | beautiful → more beautiful than | bad → worse |
| big → bigger than | expensive → more expensive than | far → farther/further |
| hot → hotter than | interesting → more interesting than | little → less |

**So sánh nhất (Superlative):**
| Tính từ ngắn | Tính từ dài | Bất quy tắc |
|-------------|------------|------------|
| the + adj + -est | the most + adj | good → the best |
| tall → the tallest | beautiful → the most beautiful | bad → the worst |
| big → the biggest | interesting → the most interesting | far → the farthest |

**So sánh bằng:** as + adj + as
- He is as tall as his brother. → Anh ấy cao bằng anh trai.
- This book is not as interesting as that one. → Cuốn sách này không thú vị bằng cuốn kia.

**Ví dụ:**
- Mount Everest is the highest mountain in the world.
- A car is faster than a bicycle.
- This exercise is more difficult than the last one.

### Lớp 8 – Units 6–10: Grammar Summary

**Unit 6: Wonders of Vietnam – Relative Clauses (Mệnh đề quan hệ)**

| Đại từ quan hệ | Thay thế | Ví dụ |
|---------------|---------|-------|
| who | người (chủ ngữ) | The man **who** lives next door is a teacher. |
| whom | người (tân ngữ) | The student **whom** I met is from Hanoi. |
| which | vật/con vật | The book **which** I bought is interesting. |
| that | người/vật | The girl **that** won the prize is my friend. |
| whose | chỉ sở hữu | The student **whose** bag was stolen is crying. |

**Mệnh đề quan hệ xác định (Defining) vs Không xác định (Non-defining):**
- Defining: không có dấu phẩy – xác định danh từ đứng trước
  - The book **that** I'm reading is a bestseller.

### Lớp 10 – Units 7–10: Grammar Summary

**Unit 7: Cultural Diversity – Clauses of Purpose, Reason, Contrast**

**Purpose (Mục đích):**
- to + V / in order to + V / so as to + V
- so that + S + can/could + V
- I study hard **to** pass the exam.
- He spoke slowly **so that** everyone could understand.

**Reason (Lý do):**
- because + clause; because of + noun phrase
- since / as + clause
- I stayed home **because** I was sick.

**Contrast (Tương phản):**
- although / though / even though + clause
- despite / in spite of + noun / V-ing
- However, Nevertheless, On the other hand (linking adverbs)
- **Although** it was raining, they continued playing.
- **Despite** the rain, they continued playing.

**Unit 8: New Ways to Learn – Relative Clauses (Nâng cao)**

**Reduced Relative Clauses (Mệnh đề quan hệ rút gọn):**
- V-ing (chủ động): The student **who is sitting** next to me → The student **sitting** next to me
- V3/V-ed (bị động): The book **which was written** by him → The book **written** by him
- to V (mệnh đề quan hệ chỉ mục đích): The first person **who arrived** → The first person **to arrive**

**Unit 9: Protecting the Environment – Inversion (Đảo ngữ)**
| Cấu trúc | Ví dụ |
|---------|-------|
| Never + have/has/had + S + V3 | Never have I seen such pollution. |

2. **Supporting Sentences** (Câu hỗ trợ): Giải thích, dẫn chứng

   - Final thought / Call to action

## PHẦN 6: READING COMPREHENSION STRATEGIES

<!-- [GRADE:6-12] [CURRICULUM:ALL] [TYPE:READING_STRATEGY] -->

### Các dạng câu hỏi đọc hiểu và cách trả lời

**1. Main Idea Questions (Câu hỏi ý chính)**
- Câu hỏi: What is the main idea/topic of the passage? What does the passage mainly discuss?
- Chiến thuật: Đọc câu đầu mỗi đoạn, tìm ý lặp lại xuyên suốt bài.

**2. Detail Questions (Câu hỏi chi tiết)**
- Câu hỏi: According to the passage, what/when/where/who...?
- Chiến thuật: Scan (lướt) để tìm từ khóa, không cần đọc toàn bài.

**3. Inference Questions (Câu hỏi suy luận)**
- Câu hỏi: It can be inferred that... / What is implied by...?
- Chiến thuật: Đọc kỹ đoạn liên quan, suy luận từ thông tin cho sẵn, không phán đoán ngoài văn bản.

**4. Vocabulary in Context (Từ trong ngữ cảnh)**
- Câu hỏi: The word "X" in paragraph Y most nearly means...?
- Chiến thuật: Đọc câu trước và sau từ đó, tìm từ thay thế phù hợp nghĩa.

**5. Purpose Questions (Câu hỏi mục đích)**
- Câu hỏi: The author's purpose in writing this passage is to...?
- Chiến thuật: Xác định tone (giọng điệu): thông tin, thuyết phục, mô tả, giải thích.

**6. True/False/Not Given**
- True: Thông tin được đề cập và đúng
- False: Thông tin được đề cập nhưng sai
- Not Given: Không đề cập đến trong bài

## PHẦN 14: COMMON EXAM ERRORS & CORRECTIONS

<!-- [GRADE:9-12] [CURRICULUM:ALL] [TYPE:EXAM_TIP] -->

### Các lỗi sai thường gặp nhất trong bài thi

**1. Subject-Verb Agreement (Hòa hợp chủ ngữ - động từ)**
- ❌ The news are good. ✅ The news **is** good. (The news – danh từ số ít)
- ❌ Mathematics are difficult. ✅ Mathematics **is** difficult.
- ❌ The number of students are increasing. ✅ The number of students **is** increasing.
- ✅ A number of students **are** absent. (A number of = nhiều → số nhiều)
- ❌ Each of the boys have a book. ✅ Each of the boys **has** a book.
- ❌ Neither Tom nor his friends was there. ✅ Neither Tom nor his friends **were** there. (theo danh từ gần nhất)

**2. Article Errors (Lỗi mạo từ)**
- ❌ She plays piano. ✅ She plays **the** piano.
- ❌ I have the headache. ✅ I have **a** headache.
- ❌ The gold is expensive. ✅ **∅** Gold is expensive. (chất liệu chung chung)
- ❌ He is best student. ✅ He is **the** best student.

**3. Preposition Errors (Lỗi giới từ)**
- ❌ She is good in English. ✅ She is good **at** English.
- ❌ I am interested about history. ✅ I am interested **in** history.
- ❌ He is tired from walking. ✅ He is tired **of** walking.
- ❌ She is afraid from dogs. ✅ She is afraid **of** dogs.
- ❌ I look forward to see you. ✅ I look forward to **seeing** you. (look forward to + V-ing)
- ❌ She is responsible of her actions. ✅ She is responsible **for** her actions.

**4. Confusing Words (Từ dễ nhầm)**
| Từ | Nghĩa | Phân biệt |
|-----|------|---------|
| affect (v) | ảnh hưởng đến | Pollution **affects** our health. |
| effect (n) | tác động, hiệu quả | The **effects** of pollution are harmful. |
| advice (n) | lời khuyên | He gave me some good **advice**. |
| advise (v) | khuyên | She **advised** me to study harder. |
| accept | chấp nhận | I **accept** your apology. |
| except | ngoại trừ | Everyone came **except** Tom. |
| adapt | thích nghi | We must **adapt** to change. |
| adopt | chấp nhận, nhận nuôi | They **adopted** a new policy. |
| economic | thuộc về kinh tế | **economic** growth |
| economical | tiết kiệm | an **economical** car |
| historic | có ý nghĩa lịch sử | a **historic** event |
| historical | thuộc về lịch sử | a **historical** novel |
| sensitive | nhạy cảm | She is very **sensitive**. |
| sensible | sáng suốt, thực tế | That's a **sensible** idea. |

### Essay mẫu: Should students use smartphones in class?

> **The Use of Smartphones in the Classroom: A Double-Edged Sword**
>
> In recent years, smartphones have become an integral part of daily life, particularly among young people. The question of whether students should be allowed to use smartphones in the classroom has sparked considerable debate among educators, parents, and students alike. While there are valid arguments on both sides, I believe that the responsible use of smartphones in education can bring significant benefits, provided that clear guidelines are established.
>
> On one hand, smartphones offer remarkable educational opportunities. Students can instantly access vast amounts of information, look up unfamiliar vocabulary, and use educational apps that make learning more engaging and interactive. Moreover, digital tools such as note-taking applications, calculators, and language learning platforms can enhance students' productivity and support different learning styles. Research has shown that incorporating technology into the classroom can increase student engagement and improve learning outcomes.
>
> On the other hand, the potential drawbacks of smartphones in the classroom cannot be ignored. The temptation to check social media, play games, or send messages can be a significant source of distraction, undermining students' concentration and academic performance. Furthermore, excessive screen time has been associated with various health concerns, including eye strain, sleep disruption, and reduced physical activity.
>
> Despite these concerns, I believe that banning smartphones entirely is neither practical nor beneficial in today's digital age. Instead, schools should implement clear policies that define when and how smartphones may be used. For instance, phones could be permitted for specific educational tasks while being prohibited during examinations and direct instruction. Teachers should also incorporate smartphone-based activities into their lessons to harness the technology's potential while maintaining classroom order.
>
> In conclusion, while smartphones present certain risks in educational settings, their potential to enrich learning experiences far outweighs these challenges when managed appropriately. By establishing sensible guidelines and fostering digital literacy, schools can help students develop responsible technology habits that will serve them well throughout their lives.

## PHẦN 17: COMMON VIETNAMESE SPEAKERS' ERRORS IN ENGLISH

<!-- [GRADE:ALL] [CURRICULUM:ALL] [TYPE:ERROR_ANALYSIS] -->

### Lỗi phổ biến của người Việt khi học tiếng Anh

**1. Thiếu "s" phụ âm cuối:**
- ❌ She walk to school. ✅ She walk**s** to school.
- ❌ The book are on the table. ✅ The book **is** on the table.

**2. Nhầm thứ tự tính từ:**
- ❌ a Vietnamese old beautiful woman
- ✅ a beautiful old Vietnamese woman (Opinion → Age → Origin)

**3. Dùng sai "the" và không dùng mạo từ:**
- ❌ She is nurse. ✅ She is **a** nurse.
- ❌ He is playing the football. ✅ He is playing **∅** football.

**4. Nhầm "make" và "do":**
- ❌ I do a mistake. ✅ I **make** a mistake.
- ❌ She makes homework. ✅ She **does** homework.

**5. Dùng sai giới từ thời gian:**
- ❌ She was born on 1995. ✅ She was born **in** 1995.
- ❌ I'll meet you at Saturday. ✅ I'll meet you **on** Saturday.
- ❌ I study at the morning. ✅ I study **in** the morning.
- **In**: buổi (morning/afternoon/evening), tháng, năm, mùa
- **On**: ngày (Monday), ngày tháng (April 3rd)
- **At**: giờ (7 a.m.), buổi tối muộn (at night), kỳ nghỉ (at Christmas)

**6. Dùng sai "say" và "tell":**
- ❌ She told "I am tired." ✅ She **said** "I am tired." / She **told** me she was tired.
- **say** + không có tân ngữ chỉ người hoặc "say + to + người"
- **tell** + tân ngữ chỉ người: tell me, tell him, tell the truth, tell a story
